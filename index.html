The Archon of Inazuma. Her demon name is Beelzebul and her true name is Ei. She wasn't always the archon, her sister Makoto used to be the ruler of Inazuma and Ei used to be her kagemusha. After Makoto's death Ei became the archon of Inazuma. Fearing the loss of all she knows she created the Raiden Shogun to take her place while she meditated to avoid erosion.

The Islands of Inazuma

Narukami Island

Narukami Island (Japanese: 鳴神島 Narukami-jima) is an island in Inazuma.
The area spans across Ritou, its port of entry, which is home to the Grand Narukami Shrine and on the coast is Inazuma City.
It is where the Inazuma Shogunate primarily takes rule, enforcing policies and laws. Inazuman public festivals and cultural events are held here.

Yashiori Island

Yashiori Island(Japanese:ヤシオリ島 Yashiori-jima) is an island in Inazuma.
At some point within two thousand years ago, Orobashi no Mikoto attacked Yashiori Island. Orobashi no Mikoto is the same god who created Watatsumi Island.
As a result of the heavy concentration of Tatarigami Island, the island suffers from torrential rainfall and frequent thunderstorms.

Seirai Island

Seirai Island(Japanese:セイライ島 Seirai-jima) is an island of Inazuma.
It is said that the island once experienced a great calamity and the center of the island was engulfed by a perpetual thunderstorm.
This storm has resulted in the unique geography of the island.

Watatsumi Island

Watatsumi Island(Japanese:海祇島 Watatsumi-jima) is an island of Inazuma.
It is one of the furthest islands and is opposite of Narukami Island.
It is known for its dense foliage and breathtaking colorswhich are distinct from plants found anywhere else.
